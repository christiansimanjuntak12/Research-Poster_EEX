# Research-Poster_EEX
Topic: **Products, liquidity, and price stability in the EEX power futures market:
Evidence from German, French, and Italian power futures**

Objective:
+ To understand the European Energy Exchange market structure, product, and market participants
+ To investigate the volatility dynamic of the most liquid European power futures namely German power future, France power futures, and Italian power futures
+ To investigate the period of economic distress impacting the future price stability on the most liquid European power futures
