# Research-Poster_EEX
Products, liquidity, and price stability in the EEX power futures market:
Evidence from German, French, and Italian power futures

Objective:
1. To understand the European Energy Exchange market structure, product, and market participants
2. To investigate the volatility dynamic of the most liquid European power futures namely German power future, France
power futures, and Italian power futures
3. To investigate the period of economic distress impacting the future price stability on the most liquid European power
futures
